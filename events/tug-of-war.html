TUG OF WAR

Test your strength and teamwork in this classic competition.

Theme

Strength & Team Effort: Show your coordination, power and unity in a game of strategy and brute strength.

Maximum Team Size

8 Members per team

Guidelines

  • Two teams will compete against each other in a bracket system order.
  • The center of the rope must align with the center marked on the ground.
  • On the refereeâ€™s whistle, both teams must start pulling the rope.
  • If the second mark on the rope crosses the center line, the pulling team wins.

Event Rules

  • Gloves, cheats or anchoring the rope are not allowed.
  • Looping the rope around any part of the body is strictly prohibited.

Rounds

  • Qualifying Round - 60 Seconds
  • Final Round - 90 Seconds

Judgement Criteria

The team that pulls the second mark on the rope past the center line wins the game.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Lowering your elbow below the knee while pulling (locking) is a foul.
  • Touching the ground for an extended period will result in disqualification.
