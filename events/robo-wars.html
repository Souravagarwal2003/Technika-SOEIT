ROBO WARS

Battle your custom-built robots in thrilling matches.

Theme

Build a robot whose task is to push the opposing robot out of the circular ring or flip it over. Different robots compete one-on-one against each other.

Maximum Team Size

5 members

Guidelines / Event Rules

  • At the start of a match, the robot must fit into a square box of side 50x50x50 cm.
  • Robot design may stretch after the match starts, but must remain a single centralized robot.
  • Maximum weight: 50 KG (excluding controls/wires).
  • Robot may be remote-controlled or manually (wired) operated.
  • Wired bots must have slack wires long enough to cover the whole track.
  • 220 Volts, 50Hz standard AC supply will be provided.
  • Teams must arrange their own adapters or batteries.
  • Maximum +24V DC power allowed.
  • Maximum 2 time-outs (2 minutes each) per team.
  • Only electrically powered machines allowed. No IC engines.
  • Bonus points if opponent is inactive for more than 20 seconds.
  • Mechanisms must not damage arena or harm people. Violators will be disqualified.
  • No restriction on mechanism type as long as rules are followed.
  • No extra time will be provided under any condition.
  • Organizers are not responsible for any loss of property, injury and delays during the event.
  • Judge's decision will be final and binding on all.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change any number of rule and the team must abide to it.
  • Participants are required to stay up to date with the website.

Rounds

Qualifying Round: (Time limit : 15 mins) Two teams will start the game, opposite teams will fight with each other. For 15 secs centre hollow dump will be opened at an interval of 1 min, if opponent is fallen out of arena or flip the robot of opponent team then those teams will be qualified for further rounds and remaining teams may be given a second change as a wildcard entry in second round.

Quarter final Round : (Time limit : 15 mins)
Final Round : (Time limit: 20 mins)

Judgement Criteria

  • The team scoring maximum points will be the winner.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Use of any parts or instruments that may harm the arena/audience is prohibited.
  • Each team must formulate their own ideas and designs. Any form of plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  • The team must adhere to the team spirit & accomplish a healthy competition.
  • Any negligence/fraud on the part of the participating team, once detected, will lead to immediate disqualification.
