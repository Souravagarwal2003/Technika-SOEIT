ART ATTACK

Mandala / Warli Art Challenge

Event Description

Let your creativity flourish as you blend traditional tribal art with futuristic concepts. Use Mandala, Warli, Gond, or other tribal art styles to design an innovative masterpiece.

Team Size & Objective

  • Team Size: Individual
  • Objective: Create futuristic innovation using Mandala, Warli, Gond art, or any tribal art form.

Rules & Regulations

  • Participants must bring their own canvas, colours, and tools.
  • Digital tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate are allowed.
  • Judging will be based on creativity, relevance to theme, and originality.
