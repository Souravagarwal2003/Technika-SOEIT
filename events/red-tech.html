REDTECH

Design and present your innovative tech-based hardware solutions.

Event Description

RedTech is a hardware prototyping challenge that encourages teams to build and showcase tech-based solutions using microcontrollers and creative engineering.

Team Size

2 - 4 Members

Objective

Design and present a functional or conceptual hardware prototype addressing real-world problems.

Rules & Regulations

  • Participants may use microcontrollers such as Arduino or Raspberry Pi.
  • A block diagram and workflow must be submitted during the presentation.
  • A working demo is preferred but not mandatory.
  • Judging criteria: Innovation, Design, Practical Utility.
