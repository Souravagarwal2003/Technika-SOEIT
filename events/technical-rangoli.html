TECHNICAL RANGOLI

Showcase your artistic skills and creativity in this vibrant competition.

Event Description

Let your imagination bloom with vibrant colors and creativity. Design intricate rangolis to represent themes that will be announced on the spot. It’s not just about art, but also about team coordination, planning, and execution.

Theme

Will be declared on the spot.

Event Rules & Guidelines

  • Maximum Team Size: 3 members (minimum 2 members allowed).
  • Students from different institutes can form a team.
  • Participants must report 15 minutes before the event starts.
  • Teams must bring their own materials for preparing the rangoli.
  • Indiscipline may result in cancellation of registration.
  • Multiple entries by a single person are not allowed.

Event Specifications

  • Rangoli area: Maximum 3 ft x 3 ft.
  • No reference material (books, phones, etc.) allowed during the event.
  • Qualifying Round: 1 hour
  • Final Round: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • No extra time will be provided under any circumstances.
  • Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
  • Stay updated via the official Technika 5.0 website.

Judgement Criteria

  • Overall appearance and appeal
  • Details and clarity of design
  • Color combination and creativity
  • Neatness

Disqualification Criteria

  • Talking or discussing with another team during the event.
  • Copying designs or ideas from other teams (plagiarism).
  • Not maintaining team spirit or fair competition.
  • Any detected negligence or fraudulent behavior.
