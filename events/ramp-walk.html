RAMP WALK

Walk the Ramp with Elegance and Attitude

Event Theme

Qualifying Round Theme: Astra – Stars on Earth

Final Round Theme: Digital Bloom

Let your outfit speak your vision of stars and technology. Shine like a celestial entity in the qualifying round and bloom like futuristic fashion in the final!

Event Description

Step into the spotlight and showcase your style, creativity, and confidence. Embrace the theme "Astra: Stars on Earth" and dazzle the runway. This fashion-forward ramp walk event challenges participants to interpret celestial beauty through performance and presentation.

Team Details

Maximum Team Size: 8 members

Guidelines

  • This will be a two-round event: Qualifying Round and Final Round.
  • Selected participants from Day 1 qualify for Final Round on Day 2.
  • Day 1: Theme performance and talent round.
  • Day 2: Final walk + Q&A based on “Digital Bloom”.

Event Rules

  • Time Limit: 5 mins (Qualifying) & 8 mins (Final)
  • Participants must report 30 mins before the event.
  • Stick to the theme for extra points.
  • Green room provided for costume changes.
  • Tracks must be carried via CD, pen drive, etc.
  • Judging Criteria: Costume, Theme, Walk, and Attitude.
  • Judge’s decision is final.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Use of cigarettes, alcohol or any unfair means.
  • Arguing with judges.
  • Any form of vulgarity or obscenity.

Key Points

  • Teams must bring their own props.
