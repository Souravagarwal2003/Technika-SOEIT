COMPUTER GAMING: NFS

Compete in thrilling races and challenges in the world of LAN PC gaming.

Event Description

Show your racing skills in an intense LAN PC gaming tournament featuring Need for Speed. Compete head-to-head using a common setup and race to victory!

Theme

LAN PC Gaming

Maximum Team Size

Individual

Guidelines

  • All players will use a common car: BMW M3 GTR.
  • Race Mode Options:
    • Nitrous: On
    • Collision Detection: On
  • Standard keyboards will be provided. Players may bring their own keyboard or controller.

Event Rules

  • Tournament will follow a knockout format.
  • Winners of the 1st round advance to the next round.

Rounds

  • Circuit (Elimination Round)
  • Sprint (Final Round)

Judgement Criteria

  • Participant completing the track in minimum time and scoring higher will be the winner.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Use of any cheat code or external program.
  • Use of foul language or slangs during gameplay.
