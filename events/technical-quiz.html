TECHNICAL QUIZ

Test your technical knowledge in science, engineering, and general awareness.

Theme

Questions will be based on topics from Science, Engineering, Traditions, Laws, and General Knowledge.

Team Composition

  • Maximum Team Size: 3 members

Event Rounds & Rules

First Round: Lap Round

  • Each team will be asked 10 questions.
  • +5 marks for each correct answer.
  • No negative marking for wrong answers.
  • If a team can't answer, the question will pass to the next team.
  • 30 seconds time to answer each question.

Second Round: Audio-Visual Round

  • Each team will be shown 2 audio-visual questions.
  • +10 marks for each correct answer.
  • No negative marking.
  • 60 seconds playtime for video, 30 seconds to answer.
  • If unanswered, the question will be passed to the next team.
  • Top 5 teams proceed to final round.

Final Round: Rapid Fire

  • Each team will answer 5 rapid questions.
  • Time limit: 5 seconds per question.
  • +10 marks for correct answer, -5 marks for wrong/no answer.
  • New question every 10 seconds.
  • Top 2 teams declared Winner and Runner-up.

Judgement Criteria

  • Speed and accuracy of answers.
  • Number of correct answers across all rounds.
  • Time taken in rapid-fire round.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Use of any gadgets during the quiz.
  • Use of unauthorized resources or materials.
  • Plagiarism or copying from other teams.
  • Unprofessional conduct or misbehavior during the event.
