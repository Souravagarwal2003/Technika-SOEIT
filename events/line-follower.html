LINE FOLLOWERS

Navigate your robot through a challenging course using line detection.

Theme

The task for line following robots is to drive through the track marked with a black line as fast as possible.

Maximum Team Size

5 members

Guidelines / Event Rules

  • Students from different institutes can form a team.
  • A team may consist of 2 to 5 members.
  • The robot must be autonomous.
  • Maximum robot dimensions: 25cm (W) × 25cm (L) × 25cm (H); Maximum weight: 1kg.
  • Robots must follow a 3 cm thick black line on a white surface.
  • The robot must always stay on the line; failing to do so results in disqualification.
  • No field damage or danger to spectators allowed.
  • Voltage limit: max 24V DC.
  • Robot must have a start/stop button.
  • Track includes inclines, zigzags, curves, breaks, etc., and robot must finish the course.
  • No extra time will be provided.
  • Organizers are not liable for loss, injury, or delays.
  • Judges’ decisions are final and binding.
  • Organizers may change rules anytime; participants must comply.
  • Participants must stay updated via the official website.

Rounds

  • Qualifying Round (2 Attempts): Best time out of 2 trials is considered.
  • Final Round (1 Attempt): Only one chance to complete the track.

Judgement Criteria

The team that completes the specified track in the shortest time wins.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Using unauthorized parts/tools provided by organizers.
  • Plagiarism in design or idea.
  • Violation of sportsmanship or misconduct.
  • Detected fraud or negligence by team members.
