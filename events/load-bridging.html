LOADING BRIDGE

Build a strong yet light bridge with ice sticks and test its strength under load.

Event Description

This is a group event focused on engineering design and creativity. Participants will build a bridge using only ice cream sticks and glue gum, then test its load-bearing strength.

Guidelines

  • This event will have two rounds:
    • Day 1: Building the bridge (3 hours time limit)
    • Day 2: Load testing of the bridge
  • Participants must bring approximately 150â€“200 ice sticks and glue gum (Fevicol).
  • Participants should carry their own scale and cutter.
  • Only group entries allowed. (2 to 5 participants per group)
  • Use of any material other than ice sticks and glue gum will lead to disqualification.
  • The bridge span length is not specified.

Event Rules

  • Report at the venue 30 minutes before the event.
  • No rods, metal pins, or any external items are allowed.
  • Only registered participants can compete.
  • The bridge will be judged based on its weight and the load it can carry.
  • The lightest bridge with the highest load-bearing capacity will win.
  • Judges' decision will be final and binding.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Failure to report 30 minutes before the event.
  • Use of unauthorized materials.
  • Exceeding the allotted time limit.
  • Arguing or misbehaving with judges or event coordinators.

Schedule

  • Day 1: Bridge Construction Begins
  • Day 2: Load Testing and Judging
