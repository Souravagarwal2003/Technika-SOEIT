INNOVATIVE SPRINT

Rapid development of innovative tech solutions in teams.

Theme

A creative and implementable solution to a real-world problem in a limited time, aligned with the Technika 2024 theme.

Team Composition

  • Team size: 2â€“4 members.
  • Participants must be registered for Technika 2024.

Rules

  • Problem Statement: Will be provided at the beginning of the event.
  • Solution Development: Teams will have 3 hours to brainstorm, develop, and finalize their solutions.
  • Presentation Structure:
    • Problem Understanding: Clear identification of the problem.
    • Proposed Solution: Detailed explanation of the innovative solution.
    • Technical Approach: Outline of technical methods used.
    • Implementation Feasibility: Practical steps for real-world application.

Time Allocation

  • Preparation: 3 hours to develop the solution.
  • Presentation: 10 minutes.
  • Q&A: 5 minutes with judges.

Submission

Teams must submit a brief report in PPT or PDF format before presenting.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation: Creativity and originality (30%).
  • Feasibility: Practical application and sustainability (30%).
  • Technical Rigor: Quality and depth of the technical approach (20%).
  • Presentation: Clarity and impact (20%).

Code of Conduct

  • On-the-Spot Development: All solutions must be developed during the event. Pre-prepared ideas will lead to disqualification.
  • Professionalism: Teams must adhere to the schedule and maintain professionalism throughout.
