IDEA IGNITION

Showcase your vision. Solve real-world problems with innovative ideas.

Event Description

Idea Ignition is a platform where teams present original solutions to real-world problems. Pitch your idea with impact and creativity to stand out!

Team Size

2 - 4 Members

Objective

Present innovative and feasible ideas to solve existing real-world challenges.

Rules & Regulations

  • Ideas must be original and not previously submitted elsewhere.
  • Teams must submit abstracts (maximum 300 words) prior to the event date.
  • Each team will get 10 minutes for presentation, followed by 5 minutes of Q&A.
  • Judging will be based on: Innovation, Feasibility, and Impact.
