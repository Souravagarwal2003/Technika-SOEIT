TECHNICAL DEBATE

Engage in thought-provoking discussions on cutting-edge technology topics.

Event Description

Participate in a stimulating technical debate where you'll be tested on your analytical, critical thinking, and communication skills. Be prepared to debate on current and emerging technological topics, presented on the spot!

Guidelines

  • Language: Hindi or English (as per participant's choice).
  • Topic will be given on the spot and will be relevant to current technical trends.
  • Speaking Time: Minimum 2 minutes, Maximum 3 minutes.
  • Participants must present clear arguments supported by facts or reasoning.

Judging Criteria

  • A. Reasoning and Evidence: Strength and clarity of arguments, logical support.
  • B. Listening and Response: Ability to understand and counter opposing views.
  • C. Organization and Prioritization: Structured flow and clarity of speech.
  • D. Expression and Delivery: Confidence, tone, and engagement with the audience.
