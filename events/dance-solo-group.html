DANCE SOLO GROUP

Showcase your moves with solo and group dance performances.

Theme

ECLIPTIC RHYTHMS: Dance through Diversity (Any dance form allowed)

Event Description

Participate in energetic solo or group dance competitions across various styles and genres. Show your passion, expression, and rhythm on stage!

Maximum 5â€“10 members per team.

Guidelines

  • No provoking dress code is allowed.
  • No abusive words should be used in the song.
  • Song must be submitted one day before the event.
  • Dress code should be proper and decent.
  • Use of props and production material is allowed and must be brought by the participants. Final permission will be at organizers' discretion.
  • Registration and song submission are not allowed on the event day.

Rounds

  • Qualifying Round:
    • AJU students: One day before the event
    • Other institutions: On the event day
    • Max Song Duration: 3 minutes
  • Final Round:
    • Selected participants only (including AJU students)
    • Max Song Duration: 5 minutes

Judgement Criteria

  • Facial expressions and body language
  • Footwork
  • Hand and eye movements
  • Costume and presentation
  • Choreography
  • Selection of music piece
  • Overall clarity and impact

Disqualification Criteria

  • Inappropriate song
  • Inappropriate dress
  • Theme not followed

Key Points

  • Theme should be properly followed.
  • Registration of participants and submission of song will not be allowed on-site.
