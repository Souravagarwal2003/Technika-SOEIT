POT PAINTING

“Nature's Palette: Crafting Eco-Friendly Art”

Event Description

The Pot Painting competition at Technika 5.0 - NOVA invites participants to showcase their creativity by transforming plain pots into beautiful works of art. Reflect the harmony between nature and innovation using eco-friendly materials and a theme aligned with nature.

Rules and Guidelines

  • This is strictly an individual competition. No group participation is allowed.
  • Participants must bring their own pot made of terracotta or clay (eco-friendly).
  • Pot size must not exceed 12 inches in height and 10 inches in diameter.
  • Only acrylic or poster colours are allowed. No spray paints, markers, stickers or decals permitted.
  • Designs must be entirely hand-painted.
  • Time limit for painting is 1 hour.
  • Theme: “Nature's Palette: Crafting Eco-Friendly Art.” Reflect nature—flora, fauna, landscapes, or eco-messages.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity and originality
  • Relevance to the theme
  • Neatness and presentation
  • Colour combination and finishing

Important Points

  • Complete painting within 1 hour.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • Use of mobile phones or reference material is prohibited.
  • Plagiarism or copying others will result in disqualification.
  • Focus on artistic skill and eco-conscious creativity.
