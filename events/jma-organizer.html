JMA ORGANIZER

Unleash your creativity, management skills, and team coordination under one roof.

Event Description

"JMA Organizer" is a unique event aimed at testing students’ abilities in planning, organizing, and executing mock academic or technical events. Participants are expected to design everything from theme, logistics, team roles, budgeting, scheduling, and promotional strategies – just like real-world event managers.

Guidelines & Rules

  • Participation is allowed in teams of 2-4 members.
  • Each team must present a detailed plan for organizing a hypothetical technical event.
  • The plan should include:
    • Theme & Objective of the Event
    • Budget and Resource Planning
    • Timeline and Schedule of Activities
    • Team Roles and Responsibilities
    • Marketing, Publicity & Outreach Ideas
    • Risk Management & Innovation Strategies
  • Teams will be judged on creativity, practicality, presentation, and time management.
  • Presentation time: 8 minutes followed by 2 minutes Q&A.
  • Teams must submit a printed proposal (1–2 pages) before the presentation.
  • Formal dress code and college ID are mandatory.
  • Judges’ decision will be final. Certificates will be provided to all participants.

Event Schedule

  • 10:00 AM – Check-In & Briefing
  • 10:30 AM – Presentation Rounds Start
  • 01:00 PM – Lunch Break
  • 02:00 PM – Final Results & Award Ceremony
