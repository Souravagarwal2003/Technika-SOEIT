STORY TELLING

Unleash your imagination and narrate captivating tales.

Event Description

Step into the world of storytelling and captivate your audience with your imagination. Choose an engaging story and bring it to life using your voice, expressions, and gestures.

Guidelines

  • Language: English / Hindi
  • Choose a story with a clear beginning, middle, and end.
  • Demonstrate good stage presence, facial expressions, gestures, and body language.
  • Use clear voice projection, proper enunciation, and expression.
  • Story duration should be between 3 to 5 minutes.
  • If the story exceeds 7 minutes, judges have the right to stop the performance.
  • Story must be memorized and not read.

Judging Criteria

  • Content selection and structure
  • Poise and body language
  • Voice clarity, projection, and modulation
  • Memorization and storytelling flow
  • Overall uniqueness and audience engagement
