FIRE FREE COOKING

Showcase your culinary skills with no flame!

Event Description

An exciting culinary event where creativity meets cold cuisine. Participants must craft fire-free dishes using raw ingredients while impressing the judges with hygiene, innovation, and presentation.

Theme

Creative Cold Cooking — All dishes must be prepared without any form of heat.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. Team Size: 2-4 members.
  2. Total Preparation Time: 1 Hour.
  3. Report at venue 30 minutes prior to start.
  4. All cutting must be done on the spot — no prior prep allowed.
  5. Milk and dairy allowed; non-veg prohibited.
  6. No readymade items (bread, biscuits, chocolates, etc.) or canned/boiled goods.
  7. No readymade decorations allowed.
  8. Get raw materials approved one day before the event.
  9. Prepare up to 3 items (one must be a beverage).
  10. Bring separate utensils (glass, spoon, straw, etc.) for 3 coordinators and 2 judges.
  11. No heating methods permitted.

  12. Allowed Ingredients:

    • Vegetables: tomatoes, bell peppers, boiled potatoes, carrots, cabbage, onions, coriander, etc.
    • Fruits: orange, pineapple, strawberry, banana, etc.
    • Cereals: brown bread, white bread, penne pasta.
    • Others: mayonnaise, cream, pesto, butter, cheese, coffee, milk, sugar, chips, etc.
    • Masalas: chat masala, salt, pepper powder.
    • Herbs: oregano, basil, mixed herbs, chili flakes, lemon, etc.

  13. No assistants allowed during the event.
  14. Carry your ID Card.
  15. Maintain cleanliness and hygiene throughout.
  16. Clean your workspace after the competition.
  17. Judges' decision will be final and binding.
