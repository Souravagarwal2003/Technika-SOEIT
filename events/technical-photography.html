TECHNICAL PHOTOGRAPHY

Capture stunning images and showcase your photographic skills.

Event Description

Submit original, theme-based photographs captured on your mobile phone. Show your creativity and technical skills through your lens.

Event Details

  • Theme: Will be declared on the spot
  • Maximum Team Size: 2 members

Guidelines

  • The photograph should depict the on-spot theme.
  • Two entries are allowed.
  • Photographs must be original with no watermark.
  • Color or black & white entries allowed.
  • Submit highest resolution possible.
  • Portrait or landscape both allowed.
  • Caption/title must be given to the photograph.

Event Rules

  • Only phone cameras should be used.
  • Photo size must be between 5MP to 20MP.
  • Photographs must relate to the fest theme.
  • Maximum of 3 best photos can be submitted per participant.
  • Submit in JPEG format only.
  • Photos must be clicked within the campus.
  • No edits or filters allowed.

Judgement Criteria

  • Originality, concept, technical excellence
  • Composition, overall impact, creativity
  • Jury's decision will be final

Disqualification Criteria

  • Digitally altered photos beyond standard optimization will be disqualified.
  • Use of cameras other than mobile phones is prohibited.
  • Improper content will lead to direct disqualification.

Key Points

  • Photos may be used by Arka Jain University with due credit to the photographer.
  • Caption/title and technical details must be provided with each photo.
