PROJECT MODEL EXHIBITION

Showcase your innovative projects related to Science, Society and Environment.

Theme & Description

The exhibition invites students to present innovative models and projects related to Science, Society, and Environment. Models can be working or demonstrative, presented by individuals or teams (up to 4 members). Participants must adhere to all display and documentation requirements.

Guidelines & Event Rules

  • Online/Offline/Spot registration of each student is required.
  • Maximum 4 members per team. One project per student/team.
  • Label your model with its name, your name, and college/school name.
  • Participants must wear College/University uniform with ID card.
  • A one-page abstract (minimum 100 words) must be submitted as hardcopy.
  • Bring all necessary project accessories and a chart with:
    • Block Diagram
    • Working Principle
    • Advantages & Disadvantages
    • Future Scope
  • Participants must remain with their project for judging and remove it at day's end.
  • Judgesâ€™ decision is final. Non-innovative entries may be disqualified.
  • Participation certificate to all. Prizes for winning teams.

Event Rounds

  • Round I: Display for 3 hours. Judged on theme relevance and presentation style.
  • Round II: Display for 2 hours. Judged on efficiency, performance, design, and application.
