ROBO RACE

Speed your bot through the maze and win!

Theme

Design a robot either wired or wireless within the specified dimensions that can be operated manually and travel through all turns of the track. The robot that completes the specified task in the least time will be the winner. Think your robot can overcome any obstacle — big or small — in record time?

Maximum Team Size

5 members

Guidelines

  • Max robot dimensions: 25cm x 20cm x 15cm (L × B × H).
  • Robot may be wired or wireless.
  • Wire (if any) must be long enough and remain slack throughout the run.
  • Maximum weight: 3 kg.
  • Power supply: 220V, 50Hz AC (adapter/batteries to be arranged by participants).
  • Use of LEGO parts or ready-made kits is not allowed.
  • Machine must be electrically powered. IC engines not allowed.
  • Batteries must be sealed, immobilized electrolyte type (gel/lithium/NiCad/dry).
  • Max voltage anywhere in the machine: 20V DC.

Event Rules

  • This is a racing event; fastest and most balanced bot wins.
  • Robot must follow all given specifications.
  • Inter-college teams are allowed.
  • All members must carry college ID cards.
  • Robot must not damage the arena or leave behind parts.
  • No second chances after the run.
  • Competition is based on time trial system. No extra time will be given.
  • Organizers are not liable for injuries, losses, or delays.
  • Judges' decision is final and binding.
  • Rules are subject to change. Stay updated via official sources.

Track Specifications

  • Track may have uneven surfaces and various obstacles.
  • Obstacles may include switch bridge, speed breakers, marble pit, slippery path, rotating disc, curve ramp down, seesaw, humps, sand, etc.
  • Exact track design will be disclosed on event day.

Rounds

  • Qualifying Round
  • Final Round

Judgement Criteria

  • Time to complete the track.
  • Number of checkpoints cleared.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Use of unauthorized parts or instruments.
  • Plagiarism or use of non-original designs.
  • Breaking team spirit or engaging in fraudulent behavior.
  • Negligence or misconduct by any team member.
