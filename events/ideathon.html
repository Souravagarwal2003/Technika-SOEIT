Event Details

  • Mode: Hybrid Mode
  • Problem Statement: Will be provided on the spot.
  • Team Size: 1-5 participants per team

Rules & Guidelines

  1. Eligibility: Open to all participants of Technika 2024. Each team must have 1 to 5 members.
  2. Problem Statement: Will be provided on the day of the event. Teams will have 6 hours to brainstorm and propose a solution.
  3. Submission Format: Teams must submit a presentation or report including:
    • Problem Identification
    • Proposed Solution
    • Innovation
    • Implementation Strategy
  4. Resources: Teams may use laptops, internet access, and provided stationery. Pre-prepared materials are not allowed.

Judging Criteria

  1. Innovation: Creativity and originality (30%)
  2. Feasibility: Practicality and applicability (30%)
  3. Presentation: Clarity and structure (20%)
  4. Teamwork: Participation of all members (20%)

Time Limits

  1. Problem Solving: 6 hours to come up with the solution.
  2. Presentation: Each team will have 15 minutes to present their solution, followed by 10 minutes for Q&A.
  3. Evaluation: Teams will be evaluated by a panel of judges from the industry and academia.
  4. Plagiarism: Any form of plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification.
  5. Code of Conduct: Participants must adhere to the event schedule, and maintain discipline and sportsmanship.
