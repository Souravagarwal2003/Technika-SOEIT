JUNKYARD WARS

Build innovative machines from scrap materials and solve real-time challenges!

Event Description

The participants will come with their random scrap material (the only inventory) out of which they have to build a working model (machine) that can perform the specified task. This is a test of your creativity, dexterity, and problem-solving skills under constraints. Thatâ€™s what Junkyard Wars is all about.

Team Size

Maximum 4 members per team. Students from different institutions can team up.

Event Guidelines & Rules

  • A team may consist of 2 to 4 members.
  • Students from different institutes can form a team.
  • The event consists of a Qualifying Round and a Final Round (on the next day).
  • All participants must report 30 minutes before the round starts.
  • Qualifying Round: A 1-hour quiz testing engineering aptitude. Approx. 5 teams will be selected for finals.
  • Final Round: Build a working model using random scrap based on a theme announced after the quiz.
  • No extra time will be provided under any circumstances.
  • Organizers are not liable for injury, loss, or damage during the event.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change the rules. Participants must comply.
  • Judges' decision will be final and binding on all teams.

Judging Criteria

The team that finishes the specified task in the shortest time with successful mobility and functionality will be declared the winner.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Plagiarism in ideas or design will lead to disqualification.
  • Lack of team spirit or unethical behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Any fraud or negligence found will result in immediate disqualification.

Schedule

  • 10:00 AM - Registration & Quiz (Qualifying Round)
  • Next Day - Final Round: Working Model Construction
