CIRCUIT DESIGNING

Design and build innovative circuits to solve complex problems.

Theme

Category: Technical

Team Structure Rules

  • Maximum 3 members are allowed in a team.
  • Any number of teams can participate from one college/school.
  • Only students are allowed to participate. Professionals are not allowed.
  • Participants must bring their valid school/college ID cards.
  • No student can be a part of more than one team.
  • Violation of any rules will result in disqualification.
  • The decision of the jury is final and binding.

Event Rounds

  • Round 1: Quiz competition. Top 3 students will be selected for Round 2.
  • Round 2: Selected students must design the given circuit on a breadboard.

Judging Criteria

  • Circuit implemented on paper without hints: 20 points
  • Circuit on breadboard with correct output: 30 points
  • Circuit on breadboard with incorrect/distorted output: 15 points
  • Each hint taken results in a deduction of 15 points

Disqualification Criteria

  • Misbehavior with judges or coordinators.
  • Violation of any team structure or event rules.
