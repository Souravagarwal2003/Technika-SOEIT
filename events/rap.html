RAP BATTLE

Showcase your lyrical skills and creativity in a melodic celebration.

Theme

Harmony

Unleashed: A Melodic Celebration

Guidelines

  • This will be a two-round event:
    • Qualifying Round
    • Final Round
  • For AJU students: Qualifying round will take place 1–2 days before TECHNIKA.
  • For School students: Both rounds will take place on the day of TECHNIKA.

Event Rules

  1. Time Limits:
    • 2–3 minutes for Qualifying Round
    • 4–5 minutes for Final Round
  2. Participants must report 30 minutes before the event.
  3. Sticking to the theme may fetch extra points.
  4. Participants must bring their own instruments if needed, and inform the authority in advance.
  5. The decision of the judges will be final.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Late reporting (not arriving 30 min before event).
  • Arguing with the judges.
  • Exceeding the time limit.

Key Point

Participants are responsible for their own instruments.
