INNOVATE

Showcase your technical creativity and engineering brilliance through live demos.

Event Description

INNOVATE provides a platform for students to present their academic or self-built hardware/software projects. Impress judges with creativity, execution, and demonstration.

Team Size

2 - 3 Members

Objective

Showcase working academic or self-built technical projects in front of peers and judges.

Rules & Regulations

  • Open to both hardware and software based projects.
  • Teams must submit a project abstract before the event.
  • A working model/demo must be presented during the event.
  • Judging criteria: Originality, Execution, Presentation.
