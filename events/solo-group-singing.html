SOLO/GROUP SINGING

Harmony Unleashed: A Melodic Celebration

Theme

Harmony Unleashed: A melodic celebration where rhythm meets soul. Express your passion for music through solo or group performances that align with the theme.

Guidelines

  • This will be a two-round event: Qualifying Round and Final Round.
  • AJU students: Qualifying round will be held 1–2 days before TECHNIKA.
  • School students: Both rounds will be held on the day of TECHNIKA.

Event Rules

  • Time Limit:
    • 2–3 minutes for Qualifying Round
    • 4–5 minutes for Final Round
  • Participants must report at least 30 minutes before the event.
  • Sticking to the theme may fetch you extra points.
  • Participants must bring their own instruments (if required) and inform the concerned authority beforehand.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Disqualification Criteria

  • Not reporting 30 minutes before the event.
  • Arguing with the judges.
  • Exceeding the allotted time limit.

Key Points

  • Participants are fully responsible for their instruments and props.
