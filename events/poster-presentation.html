POSTER PRESENTATION

Showcase your innovative ideas and research through engaging posters.

Theme

Technical Poster — The topic for preparing poster will be given on the spot. Maximum team size: 3.

Guidelines

  • Poster should be based on the given theme.
  • Title and participant's name must be written at the top.
  • Poster should present the goal in a clear and logical sequence.
  • Mention department, enrollment number, and year clearly.

Event Rules

  • No electronic gadgets are allowed.
  • Participants must bring all their required materials.
  • Reach the venue 30 minutes before the competition.
  • Winners receive prizes and certificates; all participants get participation certificates.
  • All submitted posters will remain the property of the organizing team.
  • Maintain discipline and healthy competition.

Rounds

  • Qualifying Round – 2 hours to showcase creativity. Best 8 selected.
  • Final Round – 1 hour 30 mins. Winner and runner-up declared.

Judgement Criteria

  • Innovative and creative approach
  • Relevance to theme
  • Time efficiency
  • Neatness and aesthetics
  • Logical presentation and understanding

Disqualification Criteria

  • Use of electronic gadgets
  • Sharing of materials
  • Violation of event rules
  • Indiscipline during the event

Key Points

  • Simple and clear presentation
  • Stick to the theme
  • Topic and name should be clearly written
  • Text should be concise and impactful
  • Poster should be visually appealing and logical
